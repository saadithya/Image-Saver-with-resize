Privacy Policy

Image Saver with resize Chrome Extension

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Image Saver with resize Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our extension.

🔒 Short Version: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All image processing happens locally on your device. Your privacy is fully protected.

Information We Collect

Personal Data

We do NOT collect any personal data. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Search history or visited websites
  • IP address or location data
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers

Images

We do NOT collect, upload, or store any images. When you use our extension to save or convert images:

  • Images are processed entirely on your device
  • No images are sent to any external server
  • No images are stored by the extension
  • All processing happens in your browser's local memory

Extension Settings

The only data stored by the extension are your user preferences, such as:

  • Preferred image format (JPG, PNG, or WEBP)
  • Default resize settings (percentage or custom dimensions)
  • Auto-download preference

These settings are stored locally using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.sync API and are only used to provide a better user experience. If you have Chrome sync enabled, these settings may sync across your devices through your Google account.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal data, there is limited use of information. The only data we store (your preferences) is used exclusively for:

  • Saving your default format and size settings
  • Providing quick access to your preferred options
  • Syncing settings across your devices (if Chrome sync is enabled)

We do NOT:

  • Sell or share any data with third parties
  • Use data for advertising or marketing
  • Track your browsing behavior
  • Create user profiles or analytics
  • Use data for any purpose other than extension functionality

Permissions and Their Purpose

Our extension requests minimal permissions necessary for its core functionality. Here's a detailed explanation of each permission:

Permission Why It's Needed Data Access
contextMenus Adds right-click menu options to save images No data access
downloads Downloads converted images to your device No data access
storage Saves your format and size preferences Only stores user settings
scripting Enables "Save All Images" bulk download feature Temporary page access only
<all_urls> Processes images from any website you visit No data stored or transmitted

⚠️ Important: The <all_urls> host permission is required so the extension can work on any website where you want to save images. This does NOT mean we collect or transmit data from these URLs. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Data Storage and Security

Local Processing

All image conversion and resizing operations are performed locally in your browser using:

  • HTML5 Canvas API for image processing
  • Browser's built-in fetch API for image retrieval
  • Local memory (RAM) for temporary processing

No data is ever sent to external servers or third-party services.

Chrome Storage

User preferences are stored using Chrome's chrome.storage.sync API, which:

  • Is encrypted and secure
  • Is controlled by you through Chrome settings
  • Can be cleared at any time
  • Only syncs if you have Chrome sync enabled

No Cookies or Tracking

Our extension does NOT:

  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Include analytics or tracking code
  • Set any tracking pixels or beacons
  • Use fingerprinting techniques

Third-Party Services

We do NOT use any third-party services. Specifically:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No tracking or monitoring services
  • No cloud storage or processing services
  • No external APIs or web services

The extension operates completely independently with no external dependencies.

Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any users, this is inherently protected. The extension is safe for users of all ages.

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over the extension and its data:

Access and Control

  • View Settings: Click the extension icon to view your current settings
  • Modify Settings: Change your preferences at any time through the popup
  • Delete Settings: Use the "Reset" button to clear all stored preferences

Remove Extension

You can remove the extension at any time:

  • Go to chrome://extensions/
  • Find "Image Saver with resize"
  • Click "Remove"
  • All settings and data will be automatically deleted

Clear Storage

To clear stored settings without removing the extension:

  • Go to chrome://extensions/
  • Click "Details" on the extension
  • Click "Clear site data"

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify users through the Chrome Web Store listing

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
