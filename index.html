Image Saver with resize

Download images from any website in JPG, PNG, or WEBP format with custom resize options. Fast, privacy-focused, and easy to use.

Overview

Image Saver with resize is a powerful Chrome extension that allows you to download and convert images from any website. Simply right-click on any image and choose your preferred format and size. All processing happens locally on your device - no images are uploaded to any server.

Set exact width and height or use percentage-based resizing

How It Works

1. Configure Settings

Click the extension icon to open the popup. Choose your default format (JPG, PNG, or WEBP) and set your preferred resize options. Settings are automatically saved and synced across your devices.

2. Right-Click Any Image

When browsing any website, simply right-click on any image. You'll see options to save in different formats or use your default settings.

3. Download & Enjoy

The image is instantly converted and resized according to your settings, then downloaded to your device. It's that simple!

Permissions Explained

We believe in transparency. Here's why we need each permission:

  • contextMenus Adds right-click menu options to save images in different formats
  • downloads Downloads converted and resized images to your device
  • storage Saves your format and size preferences for quick access
  • scripting Enables the "Save All Images on Page" bulk download feature
  • <all_urls> (Host Permission) Allows the extension to process images from any website you visit

Important: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing history. All image processing happens locally on your device.
